Club History:

2013-2014:
The club is formed by Gonzaga Dodgeball legend Sean Brennan! The team played in a Spokane Regional Qualifier, taking 5th place.

2014-2015:
The team wins the Spokane Regional Qualifier! Earning them a chance to play in Las Vegas! Go zags!
Click this link for an article!

2015-2016:
Chris Trigueiro is the president of year. No tournaments were played this year but it was a very chill and fun year of dodgeball!

2016-2017:
Kevin Dolan and Max Dulin are the presidents. The squad heads to the Seattle Dodgeball Tournament!

2017-2018:
Brewer Slack, Michael Shea, Celeste Hatfield, Daniel Gallab, Jack Lee and Max Dulin are the board for the year.
This is the first year that Gonzaga will play in the NCDA; being the first West Coast team to join.

The practices will still be very relaxed and fun, but the opportunity to play competitively will be an excellent option!
