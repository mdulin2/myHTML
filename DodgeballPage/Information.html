Welcome to the Gonzaga Dodgeball page! Are you ready to dodge traffic, balls and anything else that we can find!?
Club Information:

Practice Time: 10-11pm Tuesday Nights
Where: Rudolf Fitness Center, Second Court
Who Can Play: Everyone!
How competitive is it: Practices are very relaxed
Competitive Side: The team will be competing in a Northwest league for the first time this year! Teams such as Portland State, Oregon State and more!
Commitment: Low. We would love to see you at practice every week but we're not going to wring your neck for not being there.
